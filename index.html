About SHOMT

Sinethemba Hands On Mentoring & Tutoring (SHOMT) is progrmame that is designed to help, teach and educate high school and tertiary students by exposing them to practical applications of the theories being taught in class.

SHOMT focuses more on Science, Technology,Engineering and Mathematics (STEM).

SHOMT was founded by Sinethemba Lusawana who possesses a National Diploma in Electrical Engineering. His primary objective is to see students understanding and mastering the engineering related topics and able to innovate and bring their ideas into reality to solve real world problems.
