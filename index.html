About SEEDAI

Sinethemba Electrical & Electronic Design and Installation (seedai) is an engineering company whose main focus is on the design of electrical and electronic systems with significance of solving real world problems.

Our projects are user-friendly and have good protection where applicable.

Services

In all that we do we value professionalism and integrity. During the design process we apply all the neccessary laws, principles and electrical standards for design and installation. We bring ideas into reality to meet our clients' specific needs.

LV Electrical Design

Electronics Design

Direct Current circuits of 30V or less

Automation

Automating using relays and Programming languages like C.

Electrical & Electronic systems Installation

Combining LV electrical components with Direct Current electronic components.

General Electrical Maintenance

From fault finding to repairing.

Domestic Installation

Home electrical installations like Distribution Board (DB) and more.
