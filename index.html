About Sinelu

Sinethemba Lusawana Technologies (sineluTec) is an engineering company whose main focus is on the design of electrical and electronic systems with significance of solving real world problems.

Our Mission

Our mission is to solve daily real world problems by employing modern technology and science. We want to be known as one of the best electrical design companies that produce outstanding products and systems.

Our Vision

We would like to see knowledgable and enthusiastic electrical students, electricians and young electrical engineers. We want to share knowledge and skills to everyone, that is why we offer mentorship at affordable prices to unirvesity students and electrial enthusiasts.

Our projects are user-friendly and have good protection where applicable.
