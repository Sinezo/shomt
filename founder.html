Founder

Sinethemba Lusawana

Sinethemba Lusawana was born in rural Ntsimba Engcobo in the Eastern Cape. He is holding a National Diploma in Electrical Engineering obtained it from Walter Sisulu University.

He founded sineluTec with the aim of transforming his creative ideas into reality. With all his creativity he also has the aim of sharing knowledge by mentoring students to build projects of their choices from their creative minds.
