Sinethemba Lusawana

I am a qualified electrician in possession of a National Diploma in Electrical Engineering graduated from Walter Sisulu University. I have 1 year experience in working on 11kV distribution level and I am familiar with ring networks with its main components like RMU.

I have worked with Low Voltage distribution (less than 600V) and I am familiar with single phase and three phase transformers as well as mini-substations fed by overhead lines and underground cables.

Underground Fault Location

I have had the priviledge to work with High Voltage Surge Generator (Thumper) to test and locate underground cables.

Prelocated underground cable faults using a Time Domain Reflectometer (Scope).

Pin-pointed the exact location of underground cable faults using an acoustic device, or simply a ground microphone.

Pressure tested underground cables to determine whether the insulation of the cable has failed or is still in good condition.

On The Site Snaps

11kV/415V, 315kVA Mini-Substation

Have done installation and reparing faulty cables on a mini-substation. I have installed MCB and MCCB circuit breakers considering the load as well as protection of the cable.

Tesing Insulation Resistance

Tested Insulation Resistance on LV cable with 1kV rated voltage and on MV cables using a Megger.

LV Cable Joint

I have done Low Voltage cable joints on cables feeding electrical kiosks, street lights and houses.

Street Light Control System

I have designed and installed the street light control systems using a day/night photocell and a contactor.

Laying Underground Cable

Done LV joint and termination on an underground cable feeding an electrical kiosk.

Climbling Wooden Pole
